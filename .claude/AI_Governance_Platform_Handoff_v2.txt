  Clean enterprise build (NOT migrated from existing demo)
  RAGs against Product 1 catalog only
  Registers with platform on deploy — triggers live tier score
  Live governed reference implementation

BINDING CONTRACT:
  KPI definition schema links Product 1 and Product 2
  Product 1 defines what to measure — Product 2 measures it live

KEY PRINCIPLE — TELEMETRY:
  AI applications are instrumented, not aware.
  Connected apps do not know they are being governed.
  They emit standard OTEL as a side effect of normal operation.

SECTION 9 — DATA MODEL (UNCHANGED)
================================================================================

PostgreSQL owns content (what things are):
  regulation, requirement, control, application,
  metric_reading (TimescaleDB hypertable — partitioned by collected_at),
  user, division, role_assignment,
  taxonomy_term, risk_definition, canonical_risk_concept,
  risk_interpretation, interpretation_divergence_signal,
  control_metric_definition, metric_source, calculated_metric,
  control_calculation_proposal, control_tags,
  curation_queue_item, tier_change_event (immutable),
  tier_peer_aggregate (materialised, refreshed on demand)

GraphDB owns relationships (how things connect):
  Nodes: Regulation, Requirement, Control, Application, TaxonomyTerm
  Relationships (all carry RDF-star provenance metadata):
    IMPLIES, OVERLAPS, SUPERSEDES, CONFLICTS_WITH
    HAS_REQUIREMENT, SATISFIED_BY, PARTIALLY_SATISFIED_BY
    DEPENDS_ON, ADOPTS, SCOPED_TO
    EQUIVALENT_TO, SUBTYPE_OF, MAPS_TO
  RDF-star metadata: effective_from, effective_to, confidence_score,
                     asserted_by, reviewed_by, version

Azure AI Search owns discovery:
  Full-text + semantic + vector search across entire catalog
  Faceted results by domain, tier, jurisdiction, category, AI system type
  LTR reranking based on selection history (scikit-learn)

TimescaleDB (inside PostgreSQL):
  metric_reading hypertable partitioned by collected_at
